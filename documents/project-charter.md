# Calgary Housing Market Analysis

## 1. Project Overview

This project analyzes Calgary's residential housing market to identify market trends, differences between communities, housing affordability, and potential investment opportunities.

The analysis will use publicly available data from reliable Canadian sources. Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI will be used to clean, analyze, visualize, and communicate the results.

## 2. Business Context

Calgary is one of Canada's fastest-growing cities and its housing market has experienced significant changes in recent years. Population growth, economic conditions, and interest rate changes have influenced housing demand and property prices across different communities.

Understanding these market trends is important for homebuyers, investors, and policymakers who need reliable information to support better decision-making.

## 3. Business Problem

Homebuyers and investors often struggle to identify the most suitable communities in Calgary due to differences in housing prices, affordability, and market trends.

Without data-driven analysis, purchasing decisions may rely on personal opinions or incomplete market information, increasing the risk of making poor investment or homebuying decisions.

## 4. Project Goal

The goal of this project is to analyze Calgary's residential housing market using publicly available data and identify meaningful trends across different communities.

The insights generated from this analysis aim to support data-driven decision-making for homebuyers and real estate investors by providing objective information about market performance, housing affordability, and potential investment opportunities.

## 5. Stakeholders

### Primary Stakeholders

- Homebuyers
- Real Estate Investors
- Real Estate Agents (Realtors)
- Property Developers

### Secondary Stakeholders

- City Planners and Municipal Government
- Businesses evaluating future commercial locations

## 6. Business Questions

### Market Trends

1. How have Calgary housing prices changed over time?
2. How has the number of home sales changed over time?

### Community Comparison

3. Which communities have the highest average home prices?
4. Which communities have experienced the fastest price growth?
5. Which communities offer relatively affordable housing?

### Property Analysis

6. Which property characteristics are most strongly associated with housing prices?
7. Which community characteristics are most strongly associated with housing prices?

### Investment Opportunities

8. Which communities show the strongest long-term investment potential?
9. Which communities are most suitable for first-time homebuyers?

## 7. Project Scope

### In Scope

This project focuses on analyzing Calgary's residential housing market using publicly available data. The analysis includes:

- Housing price trends over time
- Home sales trends
- Community-level housing market comparisons
- Property characteristics and their relationship with housing prices
- Identification of affordable communities and potential investment opportunities
- Interactive dashboards and business recommendations based on data analysis

### Out of Scope (Current Version)

The following items are not included in the current version of this project:

- Predictive modeling and future housing price forecasting
- Commercial real estate analysis
- Individual property valuation
- Housing markets outside Calgary
